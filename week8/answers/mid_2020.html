8.1.2.1. Problem 1a¶

  • The atmosphere is 20 km thick, and has a density scale height of Hρ = 11 km and a surface air density of ρair = 1.2 kg/m3. The CO2 mass absorption coefficient is kλ = 0.15 m2/kg at λ = 15 μm and its mixing ratio is 4 × 10 − 4 kg/kg. Find the

    • the atmosphere’s vertical optical thickness τλ in the CO2 band

    • the atmosphere’s transmittance t in the CO2 band

    directly beneath the satellite.

Hrho=11000.
ztop = 20.e3
k=0.15
rho_air = 1.2
rmix=4.e-4
tau=k*rmix*rho_air*Hrho*(1 - np.exp(-(ztop/Hrho)))
print(f"tau: {tau:5.2f}")
Tr=np.exp(-tau)
print(f"Tr: {Tr:5.2f}")

Tr:  0.52

8.1.4. Problem 3¶

  • (3) A cone has a spreading angle of 23 degrees between its center and its side. What is its subtended solid angle?

Use Problem 7.2.2

\[\begin{split} \begin{align} \omega &= \int_0^{2\pi} \int_0^{23} \sin \theta d\theta d\phi \\ &= -2\pi (\cos(23) - \cos(0)) \\ &= 2\pi (1 - \cos(23)) = 2\pi(1 - 0.92) = 0.5\ sr \\ \end{align} \end{split}\]
cos23 = np.cos(23*np.pi/180.)
print(f'cos23: {cos23:5.2f}')
print(f'omega: {2*np.pi*(1. - cos23):5.2f}')

omega:  0.50
